What is Blackpink?

Blackpink stylized as BLACKPINK, is a Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment and the first girl group to debut under the same entertainment company seven years after 2NE1. The group consists of four members: Jennie, Lisa, Jisoo, Rosé. They officially debuted on August 8, 2016 with the single album Square One . Blackpink fans are called "Blink". The fandom name Blink means the fans start with Blackpink and end with Blackpink.
